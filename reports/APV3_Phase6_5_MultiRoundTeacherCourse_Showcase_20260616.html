多轮草案

轮次教师侧意图学生侧实际接收
cue_context_support先补 cue/context/target 支持普通 committed teaching step
successor_evidence再补后继分布证据普通 committed teaching step
recall_only_validation只给 cue/context 验证普通 validation case
remediate_on_failure失败后追加补习普通 committed teaching step

样例一：无教师证据仍等待

reply_tokens = ()
failure_kind = bn_not_recalled

status = awaiting_teacher_evidence
course_rounds = await_teacher_evidence
teaching_steps = ()
validation_cases = ()

没有教师答案时，多轮草案不会自己生成教学内容。

样例二：冷启动多轮课程可运行

reply_tokens = teacher::answer
failure_kind = cn_successor_weak

cue_context_support.teaching_step_count = 2
successor_evidence.teaching_step_count = 1
recall_only_validation.validation_case_count = 1
remediate_on_failure.teaching_step_count = 0

total teaching_steps = 3
validation success = true
emitted = teacher::answer

样例三：失败后追加补习

previous_failure_kind = cn_successor_weak

remediate_on_failure.trigger = cn_successor_weak
remediate_on_failure.teaching_step_count = 1
total teaching_steps = 4

失败后的补习仍然只是 AP-native evidence。

样例四：课程轮次不泄漏

student state:
  no cue_context_support
  no successor_evidence
  no recall_only_validation
  no remediate_on_failure
  no llm_policy
  no answer_table

样例五：已学会后弱补强

第一次:
  cue_context_support.teaching_step_count = 2

目标范式暴露后:
  cue_context_support.teaching_step_count = 1
  total teaching_steps = 2

边界

  • 仍不能宣称完整主动课程规划系统完成。
  • 仍不能宣称 AP 能自己生成教师答案。
  • 仍不能宣称 AP 能自己选择教师。
  • 本阶段证明的是教师侧多轮课程草案到 AP-native evidence 的最小闭环。
